PROCESS

Understand the project

The first step is to understand the purpose of the website, the target audience and the goal to be achieved. The first stage ensures that we cover the full scope of the project, make a well-defined plan, outlines specific deliverables and assign realistic timelines thereby creating a clear definition of success.

Site architecture and wireframes

As the next step we research, ideate, brainstorm and create sitemaps and flows that explain the user's journey on your website. Through detailed click through wireframes we create a high-level view of the elements on each webpage and the content architecture.

Visual design

After the wireframes are perfected, we start work on the look of each webpage. We keep the best UI practices at the center and incorporate your businessesâ€™ creative guidelines to design a website that is a seamless extension of your brand in the digital world.

Website code & development

Once the designs are approved they are brought to life by our engineering team. We use global standards and an impeccable code quality to build websites that do more than just look great. All our websites are built to have fast load times and optimised performance across browsers, devices & resolutions.

Testing & evaluation

Before the big day, the website is tested thoroughly in the production environment for missing content, broken links, responsiveness, usability, speed and performance. Tweaks are made to fix all bugs and reach the stage of final go-live.

Launch & go-live

Once the website is ready and approved by all stakeholders, it is pushed into the live environment. We keep a close watch on performance to ensure that it hits the mark and achieves the success goal.

Maintenance & upkeep

The website is an evolving asset that is placed in the dynamic environment of the world wide web. The upkeep and maintenance of website is thus an integral part of the creation process to ensure that it continues to deliver its intended purpose.
